Research Initiatives

Ongoing Research Study

Investigating the intersection of sleep, movement, and metabolic health.

Our laboratory is currently conducting an extensive study examining how circadian sleep behavior, circadian changes, and physical activity collectively influence cardiometabolic health across different stages of life.

Circadian Dynamics

Analyzing sleep-wake cycles and internal biological clocks to identify patterns that predict cardiovascular risk factors.

Physical Activity

Monitoring movement intensity and timing to see how exercise interacts with sleep quality to improve metabolic outcomes.
