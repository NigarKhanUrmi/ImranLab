Intro

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

The Cardio-DREAM Laboratory is an applied research facility within the Department of Movement Sciences that focuses on the impact of sleep and circadian behaviors on cardiovascular and metabolic health across the lifespan.

Details

The laboratory is a collaborative, interdisciplinary space that brings together faculty and students from various disciplines across the university. Through this collaborative model, we support hands-on learning and applied research experiences for undergraduate and graduate students in exercise science, health science, nutritional science, and medical education programs.
